Kansas City Riichi Mahjong

We are the Riichi Mahjong Club for Kansas City, MO and KS.


Our goal is to grow and foster the community for Riichi Mahjong and invite all to join us to learn and play.


We hold meet-ups for casual and intermediate and have a growing discord community with other Midwest locals.

Whether you are total beginner, an experienced player, or someone who loves to nerd out about Riichi Mahjong then you come to the right place.


Check out our instagram, discord community, calendar of events, and visit us at one of our meet-ups!
